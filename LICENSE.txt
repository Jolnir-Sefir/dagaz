### Responsible Use

This software implements Active Inference with metabolic selection —
a cognitive architecture whose dynamics are substrate-independent.
The same mathematics that coordinate virtual actors in a game engine
can coordinate autonomous physical systems.

The project publishes responsible use guidelines (`RESPONSIBLE_USE.md`)
and a detailed analysis of dual-use hazards (`ETHICS.md`) that
document the structural vulnerabilities, failure modes, and
architectural safeguards relevant to physical deployment. These
guidelines are not license terms, but they represent the considered
engineering judgment of the architecture's creators.

We encourage all users and deployers to read `RESPONSIBLE_USE.md`
and `VIRTUAL_ACTOR_PARADIGM.md` before deploying this architecture
on systems with physical actuators.
